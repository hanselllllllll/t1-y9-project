Impression, sunrise is a painting of a harbor in Le Havre. The place was Monets childhood and in making his art he took most of his inspiration from nature. The painting caused many things, Monet and his painting became a very important part of the impressionist movement. Many people were still unsure of this new style, with small brushstrokes that focus more on natural light rather than objects. This painting was made amidst many of Frances wars. So, it could represent the spirit of France recovering from war.

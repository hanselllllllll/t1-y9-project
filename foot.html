A footnote in writing is something that is usually skipped or often viewed as unimportant. In this song Conan is experiencing a one-sided love : “You told me that patience won't change how you felt For me” , even though many see them as a great couple : “You ate at a restaurant, the host said we're cute”. It can also be seen as being used for something just sexual but being okay with it since he is too captivated by the person : “So I'll just take a footnote in your life. And you could take my body”

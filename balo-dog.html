Balloon dog is a series of sculptures made by Jeef Koons; it consists of 5 different versions. He has become a pop icon mainly getting inspiration from Salvador Dali and the pop art movement. The art itself follows a theme from pop culture and can be interpreted in many ways, the most common view is that it symbolizes us humans. Balloons are filled with air and similarly with us, we need air just like balloons.

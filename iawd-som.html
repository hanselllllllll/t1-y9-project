A Good Girl's Guide to Murder
The song is about Mattys struggles and dangerous thoughts in life, but it is also about how he overcomes it and lives joyfully. “But your death, it won't happen to you. It happens to your family and your friends” talk over the effects our death has on our loved ones. If we don’t go forward and are fixated on the past then we can’t grow past them, “There's no point in buying concrete shoes”. To close the song off at the end of the last verse it is said that even if it all feels heavy, we just need to try our best, “If you can't survive, just try”.
